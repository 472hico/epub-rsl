# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and
expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while addressing harmful behavior directly.
- Accept responsibility, apologize, and learn from mistakes.
- Focus on what is best for the community and its users.

Harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, publishing private information, and other
conduct inappropriate in a professional setting are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently ban
participants whose behavior they consider inappropriate. Reports may be sent privately through the
maintainer's GitHub profile. Maintainers will respect the reporter's privacy and respond in a fair,
timely manner.

This code of conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).
